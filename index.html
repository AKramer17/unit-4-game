Crystal Collector



It. Is time. For. Basic addition...! ...with a twist! And crystals! Follow the instructions below:

Below you will find 4 crystals. Each crystal will contain its own numerical value, randomly determined at the start of the game.
The target number will appear on the right. Your objective is to use the number values held in each of the crystals, and continue adding the values together until you have reached the target number exactly.
Going higher than the target means you lose! Be careful as you approach the target number.
